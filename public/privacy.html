Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

We collect no personal data. Let's Go! runs entirely in your browser. Your child's name, tasks, star counts, timer settings, and PIN are stored only in your device's local storage — they are never sent to us or any server, and we have no way to see or access them.

If you purchase a Parent Settings unlock, our payment provider (Gumroad) processes your payment and receipt directly — we do not receive or store your card details. To validate your license key across your devices, we store only the license key itself and an anonymous device identifier (a random code, not tied to your name or identity). No child's name, PIN, or task data is ever sent to or stored on our servers.

We do not use cookies, analytics, or advertising of any kind.
